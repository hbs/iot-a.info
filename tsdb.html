In the context of processing IoT data, time series play a central role as most of the data is time-related, time-ordered or has a temporal component. Here are a few useful time series databases in alphabetical order along with some comments concerning usage and background:

  • OpenTSDB: a mature and established player implemented on top of Apache HBase. Scales to billions of data points and ingest rates up to the 100,000 million data points per second.
